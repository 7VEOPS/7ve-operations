🎟️ How the QR Gate Works
  • 1Guest buys ticket on your ticketing page
  • 2Platform generates a unique token per order
  • 3Confirmation email sent automatically with QR embedded
  • 4QR encodes a token URL on your Neocities site
  • 5Token validated — valid = access granted, invalid = blocked
  • 6One-time or time-limited use prevents sharing
  • 7Guest info captured: name, email, phone for your CRM
📧 Email Flow After Purchase
  • 1Instant confirmation — receipt + QR code
  • 248hr reminder — event details, what to bring
  • 3Day-of email — stream link or door time
  • 4Post-show email — archive link + membership offer
  • 5Next show announcement to all past attendees
